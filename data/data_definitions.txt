Data Definitions for Final Death Rate Data

`death_rate.csv`:
year: year
state: state abbreviation
pop: population
hydro_gms: Grams of Hydrocodone per 100k population, legitimate transactions
oxy_gms: Grams of Oxycodone per 100k population, legitimate transactions
fent_gms: Grams of Fentanyl per 100k population, legitimate transactions
unempl_rate: Percentage Unemployment (e.g. 2.5 means 2.5% unemployment)
poverty_rate: Percentage Poverty (e.g. 13.1 means 13.1% of people are living in poverty)
snap_rate: Percentage of people receiving SNAP benefits
medicaid_rate: Percentage of people receiving Medicaid benefits
gsp_per_cap: Gross State Product per Person
min_wage: State Minimum Wage
death_rate: Overdose Deaths per 100k population
log_hydro: ln(hydro_gms + 1)
log_oxy: ln(oxy_gms + 1)
log_fent: ln(fent_gms + 1)
log_death_rate: ln(death_rate + 1)
